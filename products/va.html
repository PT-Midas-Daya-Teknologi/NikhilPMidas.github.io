Orchestrating the Future of Payments & Liquidity

Advanced, cloud-native platform that transforms how businesses and consumers interact with payments.

In today's hyper-connected and rapidly evolving financial landscape, managing cash flow, optimizing liquidity, and achieving instant reconciliation are paramount. Whether for intricate corporate treasuries or high-volume digital transactions, the demand for precision, control, and efficiency is unwavering. Virtual Account Management (VAM) Solution empowers financial institutions to offer an advanced, cloud-native platform that transforms how businesses and consumers interact with payments.

VAM leverages the power of unique, non-physical virtual account numbers (VAs) that act as intelligent identifiers, seamlessly linking to a single, underlying physical bank account. This innovative approach automates reconciliation and provides unprecedented clarity and control across diverse financial operations.

VAM: Versatility Across Industries & Use Cases

VAM's robust architecture and intelligent routing capabilities make it the ideal solution for a broad spectrum of payment and cash management challenges:

Optimizing Corporate Treasury & Liquidity Management

  • Unprecedented Financial Clarity: Provide treasurers with real-time, granular visibility into global cash positions, enhancing decision-making and eliminating blind spots.
  • Automated, Seamless Reconciliation: Drastically reduce manual matching of payments to receivables, minimizing errors and accelerating cash application for various entities or invoices.
  • Optimized Working Capital & Liquidity: Centralize cash, manage multi-entity hierarchical account structures efficiently, and gain true control over working capital. Support advanced "Payments-on-Behalf-Of" (POBO) and "Collections-on-Behalf-Of" (COBO) structures.
  • Enhanced Operational Efficiency: Minimize the administrative burden associated with managing numerous physical accounts, internal sweeping, and complex reconciliation processes.

Powering Digital Wallets & High-Volume Online Transactions

  • Digital Wallet Top-Ups/Loading: Assign unique VAs to individual wallet users for instant, automated reconciliation of top-up payments, ensuring real-time crediting and a seamless user experience.
  • Automated Salary & Vendor Payments: Streamline high-volume disbursements like payroll or vendor payments by using VAs to represent individual recipients or payment batches, simplifying internal accounting and audit trails.
  • E-commerce & Marketplace Payments: Generate dynamic, unique VAs for each online order, enabling automated, real-time reconciliation of thousands of daily transactions, accelerating order fulfilment and reducing payment errors.
  • Subscription & Recurring Billing: Utilize fixed VAs for subscribers, automating the matching of recurring payments and improving reconciliation rates for predictable revenue streams.
  • Loan & Installment Repayments: Assign unique VAs to borrowers for automated tracking and reconciliation of loan installments, streamlining collections and providing instant payment confirmation.

The Unified Power of VAM: Core Strengths

  • Cloud-Native & Future-Proof: Built on Cloud Infrastructure with a flexible microservices architecture, ensuring high availability, scalability, robust data security, and continuous innovation with automatic upgrades.
  • Seamless Integration: System-agnostic design, integrating smoothly with your existing core banking, ERP, and payment systems using industry standards like ISO20022.
  • Real-time Visibility & Control: Provides instant dashboards and reports for immediate insight into cash flows and transaction statuses across all VAs.
  • Self-Service Empowerment: When paired with Banking Digital Experience, empower clients with independent management of their virtual account structures and liquidity.
  • Enhanced Security: Reduce exposure of primary bank account details to multiple payers, enhancing security and providing clear audit trails for every transaction.
