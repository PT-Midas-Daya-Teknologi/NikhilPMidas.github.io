The Enterprise Identity Fabric - NexusID

A Unified, API-First Identity & Access Management Ecosystem

NexusID is a high-performance, cloud-native Digital Identity platform engineered to centralize and secure the fragmented identity landscape of modern enterprises. By combining the extensibility of open-source frameworks like Keycloak, NexusID provides a "Single Source of Truth" for employee, customer, and partner identities.

The Problem We Solve

Most enterprises struggle with "Identity Debt"—legacy silos, fragmented MFA methods, and inconsistent authorization policies that create security gaps and frustrate users. NexusID replaces these silos with a unified, API-first architecture that scales to millions of users without compromising performance.

Core Product Pillars

  • Omni-Channel Experience: A single identity that follows the user across web, mobile, and even IoT devices, ensuring a seamless journey from onboarding to transaction.
  • Architectural Sovereignty and Agility: Unlike "Black-Box" SaaS solutions, NexusID gives you full control over your data and deployment. Run it on-premise, in a private cloud, or as a managed service within your regional borders (ensuring OJK/PDP compliance). Decouple identity from your applications. Switch or upgrade underlying systems without touching a single line of application code.
  • Rapid Integration Engine: Reduce integration time for new apps from weeks to hours. With a library of pre-built connectors for Oracle, SAP, Microsoft AD, and Spring Boot, your ecosystem stays connected and agile.
  • Zero-Trust Foundation: Shift from perimeter-based security to "Identity as the New Perimeter," ensuring every request is verified, regardless of source.
  • Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) Optimization: Consolidate fragmented legacy IAM silos into a single, high-performance control plane.

Advanced Enterprise Feature Set

Core Identity Services
  • Unified SSO & Global Logout: Seamless session management across Web, Mobile, and Legacy thick-client applications.
  • Identity Federation (The "Bridge"): Bi-directional synchronization with Active Directory (AD), Azure AD, LDAP, and Social Providers.

Standards & Compliance Mastery

  • Authentication & Federation: Fully compliant with OIDC, SAML2.0, and OAuth2.1.
  • Zero-Trust Security: Built-in support for FIDO2, WebAuthn, and hardware-backed biometrics.
  • Automated Lifecycle: SCIM 2.0 integration for real-time user provisioning and de-provisioning across the enterprise.
  • Hardened Privacy: Designed to meet GDPR, ISO 27001, and SOC2 standards, featuring immutable audit logs and granular consent management.

Target Use Cases

  • Customer Identity (CIAM): Secure digital banking portals and e-commerce apps with social login and frictionless registration.
  • Workforce Identity: Unified SSO for employees accessing internal apps, cloud services, and legacy core systems.
  • B2B / Partner Portals: Securely delegate user management to your corporate clients or partners through "Multi-Tenant" isolation.
