Democratising Banking Technology for the Institutions That Serve the Underserved

Access to financial services is a proven driver of economic empowerment, yet for billions of people across emerging economies and underserved communities, the formal banking system remains out of reach. Traditional core banking platforms — designed for large commercial banks — are too complex, too expensive, and too rigid for the microfinance institutions, cooperatives, and digital lenders that serve these populations. Our Lightweight Core Banking Platform resolves this technology gap, delivering enterprise-grade capabilities at a cost and simplicity that genuinely works for institutions of all sizes.

The Lightweight Core Banking Platform

Our platform is a cloud-native, modular core banking system that delivers everything a microfinance institution needs to operate professionally, compliantly, and at scale — without the capital expenditure, implementation timelines, or IT complexity of legacy systems. It can be deployed in weeks, configured without deep technical expertise, and scaled incrementally as an institution grows its membership and portfolio.

Full-stack microfinance platform — from mobile channel to cloud infrastructure, purpose-built for financial inclusion

The AI-Powered Digital Lending Engine

Our lending engine evaluates borrowers using alternative data sources and delivers credit decisions in real time — replacing multi-day manual processes with a fully automated application-to-disbursement journey that takes minutes, not days. This is what makes small-ticket lending economically viable for the institutions that serve it.

End-to-end digital lending in under 5 minutes — from mobile application to wallet disbursement

Key Differentiators: Digital, Inclusive, and Compliance-Ready

Group Lending and Savings Management

Many of the world's most successful microfinance models — from Grameen-style group lending to Village Savings and Loan Associations — are built on collective responsibility and peer accountability. Our platform digitises these proven models, enabling institutions to manage group formation, meeting cycles, shared liability, and individual repayment tracking entirely through mobile-first digital tools.

One field officer manages multiple lending groups — each group tracks individual member loans, savings, and meeting cycles

Key Functionalities Empowering Microfinance Institutions

  • Micro Loan Management:
    • Individual and group loan lifecycle management with flexible repayment schedules aligned to irregular income patterns — weekly, fortnightly, monthly, or seasonal.
    • Automated EMI calculation, penalty computation, grace period management, and prepayment handling.
    • Loan officer assignment, field visit tracking, and portfolio performance dashboards per officer, branch, and region.
    • Digital loan agreements, digital signatures, and paperless documentation for every loan event.
  • Savings and Deposit Management:
    • Compulsory, voluntary, and group savings accounts with configurable interest accrual and withdrawal rules.
    • VSLA (Village Savings and Loan Association) fund management with transparent member-level ledger access.
    • Term deposit products with auto-renewal and interest payout configurations.
    • Self-help group (SHG) fund tracking, contribution management, and dividend distribution.
  • Member and Group Management
    • Digital KYC and biometric member registration with offline capability for low-connectivity field environments.
    • Group formation, leader and co-signer assignment, and membership lifecycle management.
    • Meeting cycle scheduling, attendance tracking, and digital minutes capture via mobile.
    • Full relationship view — every loan, saving, payment, and interaction for each member — on a single screen.
  • AI-Powered Credit Scoring with Alternative Data
    • Creditworthiness assessment for borrowers with no formal credit history, using mobile phone behaviour, utility payment records, agricultural yield data, social network signals, and psychometric inputs.
    • Machine learning models that continuously improve accuracy as portfolio data accumulates.
    • Fraud detection and duplicate application screening using biometric and document matching.
    • Configurable credit decision rules allowing institutions to blend AI scores with relationship officer judgement.
  • Mobile-First Access and Agent Banking
    • Native mobile apps for borrowers — loan application, repayment, account balance, and statement access entirely through their mobile devices.
    • Field officer app — loan processing, collection recording, group meeting management, and GPS-verified visits, with full offline sync capability.
    • USSD and SMS interfaces for feature phone users in markets without smartphone penetration.
    • Agent banking toolkit for community-level banking agents, extending the institution's reach to the last mile without branch infrastructure.
  • Compliance by Design
    • Jurisdiction-specific KYC and AML frameworks pre-configured for major microfinance markets across South Asia, Southeast Asia, and Sub-Saharan Africa.
    • Interest rate cap enforcement and usury protection rules aligned to local regulatory requirements.
    • Central bank regulatory reporting templates, provisioning calculations, and portfolio quality reports — automated and audit-ready from day one.
    • Cloud infrastructure-level security certifications and data residency controls making enterprise-grade security accessible at MFI scale.

Leveraging the Latest Technology

  • Cloud-Native Architecture: Designed for scalability, resilience, and low total cost of ownership on AWS, Azure, GCP, or private cloud infrastructure.
  • Microservices-Based Design: Modular architecture enables rapid deployment, independent scaling, and configuration without IT complexity.
  • Open APIs and Integration Framework: REST APIs and mobile SDKs facilitate integration with payment rails, mobile money platforms, credit bureaux, and government ID systems.
  • Offline-First Mobile Design: Field operations continue without connectivity — all data syncs automatically when a connection is restored.
  • Advanced Analytics and Reporting: Portfolio quality dashboards, loan officer performance metrics, geographic distribution maps, and regulatory returns — all in real time.

Flexible Deployment and Licensing Options

  • Complete SaaS (Recommended for MFIs): Fully managed cloud environment. Go live in 2 to 4 weeks with zero infrastructure investment and minimal IT overhead.
  • Cloud Hosting (Perpetual or Subscription): Deploy on your own cloud account for full data sovereignty while benefiting from cloud scalability and resilience.
  • On-Premises (Perpetual or Subscription): Full deployment within your own infrastructure — suitable for regulated markets with strict data residency requirements.
  • Hybrid: Core systems on-premises with mobile and analytics layers in the cloud — ideal for institutions operating across mixed-connectivity markets.
