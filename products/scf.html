Empower Banks to Deliver Intelligent Supply Chain Finance Solutions with Our Cutting-Edge Digital Platform

In today's rapidly evolving financial landscape, banks need to offer sophisticated and digitally-driven solutions to meet the complex needs of their corporate clients. Our advanced Supply Chain Finance (SCF) platform provides banks with an intelligent, digital-first solution to deliver truly buyer and supplier-centric financing, fostering stronger relationships and optimizing working capital across the supply chain.

The AI-Powered Digital SCF Platform for Banks

Our solution is a next-generation, AI-enabled digital platform meticulously engineered for banks to provide comprehensive and tailored Supply Chain Finance solutions to their corporate customers. By leveraging the latest technological advancements, our platform enables banks to offer efficient, transparent, and intelligent financing options that cater to the unique needs of both buyers and their suppliers.

Key Differentiators: Digital, AI-Powered, and Buyer/Supplier Centric

  • Fully Digital Ecosystem: Our platform offers a complete end-to-end digital experience for banks and their corporate clients. From online onboarding and application processes to digital document management and real-time communication, we eliminate manual processes and enhance efficiency.
  • AI-Enabled Intelligence: We integrate Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) capabilities to provide intelligent insights and automation across the SCF lifecycle:
    • Smart Invoice Data Extraction: AI-powered Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and data extraction from invoices, reducing manual data entry and improving accuracy.
    • Predictive Risk Analytics: AI algorithms analyze historical data and market trends to provide enhanced credit risk assessment and fraud detection.
    • Dynamic Limit Adjustments: AI-driven recommendations for optimal credit limit adjustments based on real-time data and predictive analysis.
    • Intelligent Matching and Reconciliation: AI algorithms streamline invoice matching and reconciliation processes, reducing discrepancies and accelerating dispute resolution.
    • Personalized User Experience: AI-powered recommendations and tailored dashboards for both banks and their corporate clients.
  • Truly Buyer and Supplier Centric Financing: Our platform is designed to facilitate financing solutions that benefit both buyers and suppliers:
    • Buyer-Driven Programs: Enable buyers to optimize their payment terms and reduce procurement costs through dynamic discounting and payables finance.
    • Supplier-Focused Solutions: Offer suppliers access to early payment at competitive rates through reverse factoring and invoice financing, improving their liquidity and financial stability.
    • Collaborative Platform: Facilitate seamless communication and collaboration between buyers and suppliers within the platform.

Key Functionalities Empowering Banks and Their Customers

  • Invoice Management:
    • Digital Invoice Submission and Processing: Secure and efficient digital submission of invoices by suppliers.
    • Automated Invoice Validation: Rule-based validation and AI-powered data extraction to ensure accuracy.
    • Real-time Invoice Status Tracking: Comprehensive visibility into invoice status for both buyers and suppliers.
    • Dispute Management: Streamlined digital workflows for efficient dispute resolution.
  • Invoice Financing:
    • Flexible Financing Options: Support various invoice financing models, including early payment against approved invoices.
    • Automated Funding and Settlement: Efficient and secure processing of financing requests and settlement of payments.
    • Dynamic Discounting Engine: Facilitate buyer-led early payment programs with flexible discount rates.
    • Reverse Factoring Capabilities: Enable suppliers to access financing based on approved invoices from creditworthy buyers.
  • Limit Setup:
    • Configurable Credit Limits: Banks can define and manage credit limits at buyer and supplier levels.
    • Automated Limit Monitoring: Real-time monitoring of credit utilization and potential breaches.
    • AI-Powered Limit Recommendations: Intelligent suggestions for optimal limit adjustments based on risk assessment and transaction history.

Leveraging the Latest Technology

  • Cloud-Native Architecture: Designed for optimal performance, scalability, and resilience on leading cloud platforms.
  • Microservices-Based Design: Enables agility, maintainability, and independent scaling of individual components.
  • Open APIs and Integration Framework: Facilitates seamless integration with your bank's existing systems and third-party services.

  • Advanced Data Analytics: Offers powerful tools for data visualization, reporting, and gaining actionable insights.

Flexible Deployment and Licensing Options

  • On-Premises (Perpetual or Subscription): Deploy and manage the complete solution within your bank's own data centers.
  • Cloud Hosting (Perpetual or Subscription): Leverage the scalability and flexibility of cloud infrastructure (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP) while owning a license for the software.
  • Complete SaaS (Software-as-a-Service): Access the full functionality of SCF through a secure, multi-tenant cloud environment managed entirely by us.
